bonus wheat flour for north yemen -- usda the commodity credit corporation, ccc, has accepted an export bonus offer to cover the sale of 37,000 long tons of wheat flour to north yemen, the u.s. agriculture department said. the wheat four is for shipment march-may and the bonus awarded was 119.05 dlrs per tonnes and will be paid in the form of commodities from the ccc inventory. the bonus was awarded to the pillsbury company. the wheat flour purchases complete the export enhancement program initiative announced in april, 1986, it said.

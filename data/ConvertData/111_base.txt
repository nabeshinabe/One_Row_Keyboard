u.s. m-1 money supply rose 2.1 billion dlrs u.s. m-1 money supply rose 2.1 billion dlrs to a seasonally adjusted 736.7 billion dlrs in the february 16 week, the federal reserve said. the previous week's m-1 level was revised to 734.6 billion dlrs from 734.2 billion dlrs, while the four-week moving average of m-1 rose to 735.0 billion dlrs from 733.5 billion. economists polled by reuters said that m-1 should be anywhere from down four billion dlrs to up 2.3 billion dlrs. the average forecast called for a 300 mln dlr m-1 rise.
